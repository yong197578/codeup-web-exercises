Big Bend

  • In 1935, on June 20, President Franklin D. Roosevelt signed a bill that authorized the establishment of Big Bend National Park
  • The park covers over 801,000 acres of west Texas
  • The park is open 24 hours a day, seven days per week.
  • Big Bend National Park is a land of borders. Situated on the boundary with Mexico along the Rio Grande

Guadalupe Mountains

  • Guadalupe Mountains National Park is located in Pine Springs, Texas
  • Guadalupe Mountains National Park was established on 30 Sep 1972.
  • The rock exposures in Guadalupe Mountains National Park are part of one of the finest examples of an ancient fossil reef.
  • No gas, food, ice or supplies available in the park

Arches

  • Arches National Park is located in Southeastern Utah in Grand County
  • Arches contains one of the largest concentrations of natural sandstone arches in the world.
  • Arches National Park is open year round.
  • Native Americans utilized the area for thousands of years.
